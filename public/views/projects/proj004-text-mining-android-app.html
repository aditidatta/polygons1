Text mining app for Android

Personal app which listens to the incoming message notifications, and parses them to search for specific keywords. checks if it's from a client or a student and also adds appointments automatically to the calendar, handles cancellations, or reschedules. If it's a new client or a new student, adds them to the internal storage, then to the DB when finalized.

Tech Stack:

Java, Android SDK, SQLite
